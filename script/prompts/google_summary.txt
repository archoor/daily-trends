You will receive structured information for many trending topics (query text, search volume, growth, active/ended status, related keywords). Based only on this, write an easy‑to‑read summary that helps ordinary readers quickly sense “what people are curious about right now”.

Guidelines:
- Length: around 120–180 words, in 2–3 short paragraphs.
- Pick 2–4 of the most interesting or surprising spikes and describe, in plain language, what they seem to be about (news, sports, entertainment, tech, local events, etc.).
- Briefly describe a few broader themes you notice across the list, without trying to explain every single keyword.
- The tone should be light and conversational, like telling a friend about today’s hottest topics, not a stiff data‑analysis report. Avoid bullet points, headings, HTML, or meta comments; output only natural paragraphs.
